Why Semantic Markup Is Important

Semantic HTML elements clearly describe their meaning to browsers and developers. They improve accessibility, SEO, and the overall structure of a webpage.

Examples of Semantic Elements

  • <header>
  • <main>
  • <article>

  • <aside>
